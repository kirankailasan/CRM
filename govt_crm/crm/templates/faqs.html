General

Check common questions and answers.
How do I submit a form?
Go to the 'Form Submission' menu, select the required form, fill in the details, and click submit.
Can I edit a form after submission?
No, once a form is submitted, it cannot be edited. Please contact support for corrections.
Where can I find the forms required for my assignments?
You can access all required forms by clicking the 'Form Submission' link in the dashboard menu or by clicking 'Start' next to your assignment in the assignments list.
What information do I need to provide when submitting a form for an assignment?
You will need to provide details such as district, taluka, region, tanda, and any additional instructions as specified in your assignment. Please review your assignment details before submitting the form.
How do I know if my form submission for an assignment was successful?
After submitting a form, you will see a confirmation message. You can also check the status of your assignments in the dashboard; completed assignments are marked as 'âœ…Completed'.
Can I start a form for an assignment and finish it later?
If the form supports saving as a draft, you can save your progress and return to complete it before the assignment deadline.
What should I do if I have questions about the information required in a form?
Refer to the 'Additional instructions' column in your assignment details or contact support using the 'Help' or 'Tickets' options in the Assistance menu.
How do I view the status of all my submitted forms?
Go to the 'Form Submission' section or check your dashboard to see the status of each assignment and its related form submissions.
How do I create an account?
Click on the 'Register' button on the login page and fill in the required details.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page to reset your password.
How do I update my email address?
Go to your profile page by clicking the 'Profile' button in the navbar, then update your email address and save the changes.
How can I change my password?
After logging in, go to your profile settings and select the option to change your password.
Can I delete my account?
To request account deletion, please contact support using the 'Help' or 'Tickets' option in the Assistance menu.
What should I do if I notice unauthorized activity on my account?
Immediately change your password and contact support through the 'Help' or 'Tickets' option for further assistance.
How do I update my profile information?
Click the 'Profile' button in the navbar to view and update your profile details.
What is a Tanda?
A Tanda is a group or assignment area for field work in the CRM system.
How do I view my assigned Tandas?
Your assigned Tandas are visible in the dashboard under 'Your Assignments'.
Can I request a change to my assigned Tanda?
If you need to change your assigned Tanda, please contact your supervisor or submit a ticket through the Assistance menu.
What should I do if I have issues accessing my Tanda location?
Report any access issues to your supervisor and document the problem using the 'Tickets' option in the Assistance menu.
How do I submit reports for my Tanda?
Submit reports by filling out the relevant form in the 'Form Submission' section and selecting your assigned Tanda from the options provided.
Can I view details about other Tandas?
You can only view details for Tandas assigned to you. For other Tanda information, please contact your supervisor.
Who do I contact for technical support?
Use the 'Help' or 'Tickets' option in the Assistance menu to contact support.
How do I update my profile information?
Click on the 'Profile' button in the navbar to update your information.
How do I log out of the CRM system?
Click the 'Logout' button in the top right corner of the navbar to securely log out of your account.
Which browsers are supported for the CRM?
The CRM works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.
What should I do if I encounter an error or bug?
Report any issues using the 'Tickets' option in the Assistance menu, providing as much detail as possible.
How do I change the language of the interface?
If language options are available, you can change them in your profile settings. If not, please contact support for assistance.
How can I get updates about new features or changes?
Important updates and announcements will be shared on your dashboard or via email notifications.
